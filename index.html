AI Research

I am currently focused on research in AI for Science. My areas of interest span physical scales, from weather and climate science to materials discovery and molecular biology. Recent publications:

  • Accelerating the generation of molecular conformations with progressive distillation of equivariant latent diffusion models. Generative and experimental perspectives for biomolecular design workshop, ICLR 2024. [arxiv][github]
  • AdsorbRL: deep reinforcement learning for inverse catalyst design. AI for accelerated materials design workshop, NeurIPS 2023. [arxiv][github]
  • ClimateX: do LLMs accurately assess human expert confidence in climate statements? Tackling climate change with machine learning workshop, NeurIPS 2023. [arxiv][github]
  • Extracting molecular properties from natural language with multimodal contrastive learning. Computational biology workshop, ICML 2023. [arxiv][github]
  • Improving extreme weather events detection with light-weight neural networks. Tackling climate change with machine learning workshop, ICLR 2023. [arxiv][github]
I've served as reviewer or on the program committees for various NeurIPS and ICLR workshops on climate, life and material sciences, and foundation models for biological discovery.
